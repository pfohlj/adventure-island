
Three playing cards in a row. 
Can you name them with these clues?
There is a two to the right of a king.
A diamond will be found to the left of a spade.
An ace is to the left of a heart.
A heart is to the left of a spade.
Now, identify all three cards.

1. King of Clubs, Three of Spades, Ace of Diamonds.
2. Four of Hearts, Ace of Clubs, Ace of Diamonds.
3. Ace of Diamonds, King of Hearts, Two of Spades.
4. Queen of Spades, Ten of Hearts, Four of Diamonds.
